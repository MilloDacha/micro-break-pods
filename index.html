What's Inside the Pod

Every feature earns its place.

Designed around real student behavior — not an idealised version of wellness.

5 & 10 Minute Timer

A defined session length removes guilt and legitimises the break. Walk in, set your time, walk out refreshed.

Addresses: guilt & open-ended breaks

Acoustic Separation

Semi-enclosed walls and sound-absorbing foam panels create a private, quiet pocket near academic blocks.

Addresses: noise & social exposure

Warm Ambient Lighting

Soft, adjustable light reduces sensory overload and creates a calming atmosphere distinct from harsh study areas.

Addresses: sensory fatigue

Optional Breathing Audio

Guided breathing prompts or nature soundscapes help students shift from a stressed to a calm mental state faster.

Addresses: mental decompression

Ergonomic Seating

A comfortable chair angled for relaxation — not a desk chair. The posture itself signals the body to switch off.

Addresses: physical comfort

Charging Port

A USB-A and USB-C outlet. Students don't have to choose between recovery and keeping their devices alive.

Addresses: practical convenience
